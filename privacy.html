Privacy Policy

BudgetMein ("we", "our", "us") operates a local vegetarian food delivery service in Jahangir Puri, Delhi. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

By using BudgetMein, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you use BudgetMein, we collect the following information:

  • Name — provided during account creation via Google Sign-In
  • Phone number — provided when placing an order
  • Delivery address — entered manually or captured via GPS
  • Precise location (GPS) — collected when you place an order to assist in delivery address entry. This is used only at the time of ordering and is not tracked continuously.
  • Order details — items ordered, order amount, payment method
  • Device identifiers — collected automatically by Firebase for authentication and analytics purposes
📍 Location Data: Our app requests access to your device's GPS location when you place an order. This is used solely to help fill your delivery address. We do not track your location in the background or at any other time.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your food orders
  • To send order confirmations and delivery updates
  • To contact you regarding your order if needed
  • To improve our service and app experience
  • To prevent fraud and ensure security

3. Third-Party Services

BudgetMein uses the following third-party services that may collect data as part of their operation:

Google Firebase — We use Firebase for user authentication, database storage, and app security. Firebase may collect device identifiers, usage data, and crash reports. Firebase Privacy Policy →

Google Sign-In — We use Google OAuth for account creation and login. Google's privacy policy applies to data collected through this service. Google Privacy Policy →

Telegram — Order notifications are sent to our internal team via Telegram Bot API. Only order-related information is transmitted. No user-identifiable data is shared publicly. Telegram Privacy Policy →

Cloudinary — We use Cloudinary to host and serve app images (e.g., food photos, app icons). Cloudinary Privacy Policy →

Vercel — Our app is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect standard server logs including IP addresses. Vercel Privacy Policy →

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third party. Your data is shared only in the following situations:

  • With our delivery partners — only your name, phone, and delivery address, for the purpose of delivering your order
  • With third-party services listed above, as part of their normal operation
  • If required by law or legal proceedings
✓ We never sell your data ✓ No advertising networks ✓ No data brokers

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for up to 2 years from the date of your last order or account activity, whichever is later. After this period, your data is automatically deleted from our systems.

Order history records may be retained for up to 5 years for accounting and legal compliance purposes, in an anonymized form where possible.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

BudgetMein uses browser localStorage to store your cart, session information, and app preferences on your device. This data is stored locally and is not transmitted to our servers. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking cookies.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including Firebase Security Rules, App Check (reCAPTCHA v3), HTTPS encryption, and secure serverless proxies. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at budgetmeinhelpdesk@gmail.com and we will delete such information promptly.

9. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at budgetmeinhelpdesk@gmail.com or WhatsApp +91 96258 67683. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

3. Pricing and Fees

  • All prices are listed in Indian Rupees (₹) and are inclusive of applicable taxes

BudgetMein currently accepts Cash on Delivery (COD) only. Please keep exact change ready. We do not accept UPI, cards, or any digital payment at the door at this time.

  • You may cancel your order within 2 minutes of placing it, before it is accepted by our team
