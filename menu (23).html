1. Who We Are

BudgetMein is a local vegetarian food delivery service based in Jahangir Puri, Delhi – 110033. We operate as a home-based food delivery business, buying food from trusted local restaurants and delivering it fresh to your door. Contact: +91 96258 67683  |  Available: 3:00 PM – 9:00 PM, Mon–Sun.

2. Information We Collect

When you sign up or place an order, we collect:

  • Full Name — to personalize your experience
  • Phone Number — to contact you about delivery
  • Email Address — for account login and password reset
  • Delivery Address — to deliver your order to the right location
  • Order History — to show you your past orders
  • Feedback & Ratings — to improve our food and service

  • To process and deliver your food orders
  • To send order status updates and notifications

  • To improve our menu, food quality, and delivery based on feedback
  • To send you promotional offers (only if you opt in)

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is securely stored using Google Firebase (a trusted Google service). We use industry-standard security practices. We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is used solely to provide BudgetMein services.

5. Your Rights

  • Update your name, phone, and address anytime from Profile (👤 icon)
  • Delete your order history from My Orders section
  • Request full account deletion by calling/WhatsApp-ing us
  • Opt out of promotional messages anytime

6. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage to save your cart, address, and session data for a smooth ordering experience. No tracking cookies are used.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
